## Monthly Evaluation Checklist

Once a month, go through this. Be honest — this is for you, not for anyone else.

**HABIT STATUS CHECKLIST**

**Habit: _____________________________**

- [ ] I completed this habit ____ days this month (target: 20+)
- [ ] My average rating for this habit is ____ / 10
- [ ] I'm still excited about this habit [ ] Yes [ ] Sort of [ ] Not really
- [ ] This habit feels like part of who I am [ ] Yes [ ] Getting there [ ] No
- [ ] The "why" still resonates [ ] Yes [ ] Needs refresh [ ] Lost

**If you checked "Not really" or "No" on 3+ items, ask yourself:**
- Is this the right habit for me right now?
- Does it need to be smaller?
- Should I pause and try again later?

## Environment Design Checklist

Your environment determines your behavior more than you think. Design it on purpose.

**For each habit, ask yourself:**

- [ ] Is the necessary equipment visible and accessible?
- [ ] Is there anything in my environment that triggers the BAD habit?
- [ ] Can I make the good habit the path of least resistance?
- [ ] Have I removed friction from the desired behavior?
- [ ] Have I added friction to the undesired behavior?

**Examples:**
- Want to exercise in the morning? Lay out workout clothes the night before
- Want to stop eating junk food? Don't keep it in the house
- Want to read more? Put a book on your pillow

## 30-Day Starter Challenge

Week 1: The Foundation
- [ ] Choose ONE habit (no more!)
- [ ] Write your Habit Contract
- [ ] Set your minimum version (keep it tiny)
- [ ] Design your environment
- [ ] Complete your habit 7 days in a row (even if tiny)

Week 2: The Dip Survival
- [ ] Push through days 8-14 (this is where most quit)
- [ ] Use "shrink the habit" when motivation dips
- [ ] Write your "Future Self" letter
- [ ] Start a habit stack if applicable
- [ ] Complete your habit 7 more days

Week 3: Building Identity
- [ ] Ask yourself: "What kind of person does this?"
- [ ] Start acting like that person
- [ ] Celebrate 21 days of showing up
- [ ] Find an accountability partner if you haven't
- [ ] Complete your habit 7 more days

Week 4: Locking It In
- [ ] Review your Weekly Review Questions
- [ ] Notice how different you feel
- [ ] Plan how to maintain after day 30
- [ ] Add a second habit ONLY if the first is automatic
- [ ] Celebrate completing 30 days!

## 90-Day Deep Dive Challenge

For those who want to go deeper. This is for transforming your identity, not just your behavior.

**Month 1: Choose & Commit**
- [ ] Choose 1-2 habits max
- [ ] Complete the 30-Day Starter Challenge
- [ ] Identify obstacles before they appear
- [ ] Build your support system
- [ ] Track every single day

**Month 2: Optimize & Adjust**
- [ ] Weekly Reviews every week
- [ ] Tweak your systems based on what's working
- [ ] Increase your habit slightly (if it feels easy)
- [ ] Add accountability check-ins
- [ ] Start noticing identity shifts

**Month 3: Integrate & Expand**
- [ ] Monthly Evaluation at day 60
- [ ] These habits now feel like "who I am"
- [ ] Consider adding one more habit (carefully)
- [ ] Plan how to maintain for life
- [ ] Celebrate 90 days of transformation!
